Marvel's Black Panther

Meet Black Panther, a fascinating character from the Marvel Cinematic Universe. T'Challa is the King and protector of Wakanda, a nation rich in Vibranium which is the strongest metal in the world. This metal gives the panther his powers and also makes Wakanda the wealthest nation in the world. He teams up with the Avengers to take down threats to the world.
